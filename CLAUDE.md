# Project Rules

- CTA is always "Reservation Request" (English only). For Spanish, Dutch, Portuguese, and any other language, use the natural local equivalent verb so the site reads naturally to native speakers — keep existing translations as-is (e.g., "Reservar" / "Reserveren" / "Reservar"). Do not back-translate the English CTA literally into other languages.
- Fleet includes ONLY these 6 vehicles, in this order: Compact, Sedan, SUV, Large SUV, Jeep 4x4, Van
- Never use the terms "Crossover," "SUV Crossover," "7-Seat SUV," "10-Seat Van," or "SUV Van" (the Large SUV is positioned as a premium 7-seater — refer to it as "Large SUV" only)
- All vehicles are automatic; no brand names
- Navigation uses text wordmark only; include Phone + WhatsApp in nav
- Delivery line: "We deliver anywhere"
- No OCC watermarks
- Minimum rental age: 23
- No smoking policy fee: $250
- Vehicle cards display: vehicle category + "From $X/day" (using low season rate) + small benefits line ("3-day minimum · Free delivery · Unlimited mileage"). The "From" prefix and "/day" suffix are required together — they signal seasonal pricing. Do not show 3-day or 7-day totals on cards.

# Pricing & Policies

- Low season (Apr 15 – Dec 14) daily rates: Compact $45, Sedan $60, SUV $85, Large SUV $110, Jeep 4x4 $130, Van $110
- High season (Dec 15 – Apr 15) daily rates: Compact $60, Sedan $80, SUV $110, Large SUV $155, Jeep 4x4 $190, Van $145
- Always show "From $X/day" using LOW SEASON prices on vehicle cards
- 3-day minimum rental on all vehicles
- Free delivery anywhere on the island
- Unlimited mileage on all rentals
- Always display the three benefits ("3-day minimum · Free delivery · Unlimited mileage") on every vehicle card in the local language
